SchezwanNoodles

Ingredients chicken, Yogurt, GingerGarlicPaste, RedChiliPowder, TurmericPowder, GaramMasala, Salt to taste, Butter, Onions, HeavyCream, CorianderPowder, CuminPowder,
Recipe:
In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Add chicken pieces and coat them well with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours, or overnight for better flavor. Heat butter and oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is browned and cooked through. Pour in heavy cream and mix well. Add honey or sugar for sweetness (optional). Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the flavors meld together.
